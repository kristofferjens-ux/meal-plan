Sunday Prep = Easy Week
Spend 60–90 min Sunday: cook the lentil soup, a big pot of brown rice, and your oat base. This eliminates most weekday lunch decisions and saves you ~$40+ vs. buying lunch out.
🐟
Salmon Strategy
Buy a larger salmon fillet and portion it yourself — much cheaper than two small fillets. Freeze one half if needed. Wild-caught sockeye from T&T or Superstore is the best value in Vancouver.
🫙
Canned Wins
Canned sardines, chickpeas, lentils, and white beans are among the cheapest anti-inflammatory foods you can buy. Don't overlook sardines — they're one of the best omega-3 sources available.
🌿
Anti-Inflammatory Core
This plan is built around the science: omega-3s (salmon, sardines, walnuts), polyphenols (blueberries, olive oil), curcumin (turmeric), and fiber (lentils, chickpeas, oats) — all shown to reduce inflammatory markers.
❄️
Freeze What You Can
The lentil soup and chickpea curry both freeze beautifully. If you're tired of eating the same thing, freeze half on Sunday and pull it out in 3–4 weeks. Label with masking tape.
🏪
Vancouver Shopping Tips
T&T Supermarket is unbeatable for ginger, bok choy, tamari & coconut milk. Real Canadian Superstore for bulk grains and pantry staples. Buy walnuts & flaxseed in bulk at Bulk Barn to save significantly.
